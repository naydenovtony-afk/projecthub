Everything you need to know about ProjectHub

ProjectHub is an AI-powered project management platform designed for academic, corporate, and EU-funded projects. It combines task management, team collaboration, document storage, and AI-assisted features to help teams work more efficiently. Simply create a project, add team members, organize tasks, and let our AI assistant help you streamline your workflow.

Yes! ProjectHub offers a free plan that includes core features like project management, task tracking, and team collaboration. You can start using ProjectHub immediately with our demo mode, or create a free account to save your data. Premium features like advanced AI assistance and unlimited storage are available in paid plans.

Absolutely. We take security seriously. ProjectHub uses industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage, implements row-level security, and complies with GDPR regulations. Your project data is stored securely with Supabase, and we never share your information with third parties. You maintain full control over your data at all times.

The free plan allows up to 5 team members per project. Need more? Our Pro plan supports unlimited team members, perfect for larger organizations and complex projects. You can easily upgrade your plan at any time as your team grows.

Our AI assistant, powered by Claude, can help you with task management suggestions, project planning advice, deadline reminders, risk assessment, and answering questions about your projects. It learns from your project context to provide personalized recommendations and can even help draft project documentation.

Yes! ProjectHub includes secure file storage for all your project documents, images, and files. You can categorize files as deliverables, reports, or general documents. The free plan includes 1GB of storage, with higher limits available in premium plans. All files are securely stored and accessible to authorized team members.

Absolutely! ProjectHub features real-time team chat for each project, allowing team members to communicate instantly. You'll receive notifications when tasks are updated, deadlines approach, or team members mention you. All communication is context-aware and tied to specific projects.

ProjectHub is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. Access your projects anywhere, anytime through your web browser. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are currently in development and will be available soon.
