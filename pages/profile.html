About ProjectHub
ProjectHub v1.0.0

ProjectHub is a comprehensive project management platform designed for academic, corporate, and EU-funded projects. Streamline your workflow with powerful features for task management, file sharing, and team collaboration.

Help & Support

Click the "New Project" button in the Projects page. Fill in the project details including title, type, description, and dates. Click "Create Project" to save.

Open the project, go to the Team tab, and click "Add Member". Enter their email address and set their permission level (Viewer, Contributor, or Editor).

Go to the Files tab in your project. Click "Upload Files" or drag and drop files directly into the upload area. Supported formats: PDF, DOC, XLS, images (max 10MB each).
